Mary Wolcott of Windsor CT

2nd daughter of Henry and Elizabeth Wolcott of Windsor CT.

(1) Henry Wolcott; m. Elizabeth Saunders.
(2) Mary Wolcott; b. 1622 Tolland England, d. 16 Sep 1689 Windsor CT. Came to America with her sister and brother about 1635. m. Job Drake 25 Jun 1646.(1623-16 Sep 1689), Son of John Drake & Elizabeth Rogers.
(3) Abigail Drake; b. 28 Sep 1648 Windsor, CT d. 17 Nov 1696 Windsor CT.
(3) Mary Drake; b. 12 Dec 1649 Windsor, CT d. 19 Oct 1681 Windsor CT.
(3) Job Drake; b. 28 Mar 1652 Windsor, CT d. 9 Nov 1711 Windsor CT. m. Elizabeth Clark (28 Oct 1651-22 Dec 1729), 13 Sep 1677 in Windsor CT, daughter of Daniel Clark and Mary Newberry. Elizabeth Clark m. (1) Moses Cook, son of Aaron Cook and Mary Ford (16 Dec 1645-1676, killed by natives in Westfield, MA) 25 Nov 1669.
(4) Sarah Drake; b. 10 May 1686 Windsor, CT d. 20 Feb 1697-20 Jan 1748 Windsor CT. m. Roger Wolcott (4 Jan 1679-17 May 1767), son of Simon Wolcott and Martha Pitkin, 3 Dec 1702.
(3) Elizabeth Drake; b. 14 Nov 1654 Windsor, CT d. 20 Feb 1697 Windsor CT.
(3) Joseph Drake; b. 16 Apr 1657 Windsor, CT d. 22 May 1664 Windsor CT.
(3) Hepzibah Drake; b. 12 Dec 164914 Jul 1659 Windsor, CT d. 22 Jan 1685 Windsor CT. m. Thomas Paker 23 Aug 1681.
(3) Hester Drake; b. 10 Oct 1662 Windsor, CT d. 19 Feb 1702 Windsor CT.
