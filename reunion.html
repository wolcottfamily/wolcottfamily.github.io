2026 Reunion in Denver, Colorado Preview

We are excited to gather in 2026, in Denver, the capital and heart of colorful Colorado. Look forward to seeing both new and familiar faces from Friday, July 31 through Sunday, August 2. Come early and stay late if you want to explore the nearby Rocky Mountains, relax in the mineral-rich waters of natural or resort-style hot springs, and so much more!
There are several incredibly scenic towns nearby, like Georgetown, which has a train that runs to Silver Plume and offers a tour of our very own Silver Mines. You can even pan for gold! Try riding the miniature railway at Tiny Town, a miniature village featuring over 100 1/6 scale buildings. Estes Park, just north of Denver, is home to lots of adventures and the Stanley Hotel, which directly inspired the novel and movie The Shining. The Stanley offers a hedge maze, historic tours, and recently added a captivating series of underground magic shows.
To the south, you can explore the Manitou Cliff Dwellings, Garden of the Gods, and the Colorado Renaissance Festival. Visit the Cheyenne Mountain Zoo for unforgettable up-close interactions with hippos, goats, giraffes, and budgies, and ride the Sky Ride up the mountain for panoramic views of Colorado Springs and the Eastern Plains. You do not need to wait for Santa to visit the malls later in the year; you'll find him in his cabin at an attraction-filled workshop at the North Pole in Cascade, Colorado.
So whether you catch a concert at the stunning natural amphitheater, Red Rocks, shop the artisan goods of the outdoor Pearl Street Mall, hike, swim, paddle, or boat on the abundant lakes and natural spaces, or exhilarate your taste buds with the countless craft breweries, wineries, and distilleries grown here, we can't wait to see you! Tera Row (teruwah at gmail.com) is our Reunion Host Committee Chair.
